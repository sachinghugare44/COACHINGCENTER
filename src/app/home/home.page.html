Welcome to Our Offline Tuition Classes

Welcome to Leelavati Classes, a trusted name in education since 2006. We specialize in coaching 9th and 10th standard students for all subjects, with special focus on Maths, Science, and English. Unlike large coaching institutes, we offer a personalized, distraction-free, and result-oriented offline learning environment. Our goal is to help students understand concepts thoroughly, develop exam-ready skills, and build confidence for board exams — all through regular practice, doubt clearing, and small group attention.

About Us....


Leelavati Classes is a well-established coaching institute dedicated to students of 9th and 10th standard. With a rich history of academic excellence, the class is known for its individual attention to every student, ensuring that each learner receives the support and guidance they need to succeed. The class is backed by a team of experienced and dedicated faculty members who are passionate about teaching. Their systematic approach includes regular test series, personalized observation, and continuous feedback to monitor student progress effectively. What sets Leelavati Classes apart is its commitment to nurturing all types of learners — from high achievers to those who may initially struggle with academics. Through interactive teaching methods, special doubt-solving sessions, and targeted guidance, the class helps every student develop a genuine interest in learning. Whether a student is excelling or needs extra motivation, Leelavati Classes is focused on bringing out the best in each individual.
